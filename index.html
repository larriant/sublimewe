Welcome to sublimewe

sublimewe is a social technology for birthing new life harmonious human cultures that are deeply entwined with land, lineage and ancestory. It's a we-space techonology for creating groups who can tap into the deep resources of their collective intelligence. It does this by allowing them resolve and integrate any differences between the participants whether they are intellectual or coming from the heart.

"sublimewe outlines in substantial detail a program of radical reconstruction, presenting a vision that draws from a rich tradition of thought and practice of the libertarian left and popular movements, but adding novel critical analysis and specific ideas and modes of implementation for constructive alternatives. It merits close attention, debate, and action."
