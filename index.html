Welcome to Deeper Reflexive Living

sublimewe is a social technology for birthing new life harmonious human cultures that we call 'Deeper Reflexive Living'. We use this we-space technology to create groups who have enough coherency to act together and bring their vast collective intelligence to bear on the world. The members of these groups become deeply intertwined with each other, with the land, with lineage and with ancestory.

This website is just the beginnning. In the following months we will working towards running a week long residential retreat using this modality. We will also be producing materials so you can do this at home!

Please join our newsletter below to sign up to hear about our first retreats!
